About Me


Greetings,
My name is Chimara Okeke, first name is pronounced as tchi-maa-raa. I was raised by two amazing, selfless, parents in Enfield, London. When I came of age, I was sent to school in my home country for cultural immersion. School there was unique, to say the least. Students were prohibited from owning devices: phones, Nintendos, or laptops; you name it. All we had were the computers provided by the school. This digital drought left me yearning to learn more about technology. With each passing school year, I grew more eager to delve into all the information I had missed out on once I got to college. Granted, my time at secondary school was beautiful and introspective. I had made true friends, sweet memories, and a better understanding of myself.

Once I began college, I researched to find the most fitting major to quench my technological curiosity. I settled upon Information Technology. The field of IT is boundless and difficult to tame if you do not narrow down your interests. As such, I searched within myself for virtues I hold dear. I discovered the following: design, communication, relationships, collaboration, problem-solving, and creativity. By far, I found IT consulting to be the best fit for my bevy of virtues. Much like technology, people's interests morph. I may not feel as ecstatic about IT consulting in three years as I do now, but I choose to live in the present. And the present "me" is all about IT consulting!
